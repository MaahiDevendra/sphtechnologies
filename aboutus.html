WHY Work With US?

We aim to simplify making documents accessible.

  • 99% Accuracy
  • Quick Turnaround
  • Simple Pricing

We help you meet your compliance and manage risk as per ADA, Section 508 or Affordable Healthcare Act. We ensure that your documents are converted into an Accessible PDF, Accessible Word Document that fully meets the accessibility standards and guidelines.

Our remediation services include:

  • Verification that all document metadata is included and correctly formatted
  • Proper tagging for accurate navigation by adaptive devices
  • Creation of clear, descriptive alt text for graphics, tables, charts, and images written by highly skilled subject-matter-experts
  • Repair of broken, missing or inappropriately formatted links
  • Correct accessible formatting of tables, charts and forms
  • Easy-to-understand accessibility validation report for every document
  • Reducing the challenges and providing a solution to make documents accessible
  • Assuring customers that these documents meet the accessibility standards by going beyond the checklist and testing with screen readers users
  • Building inclusion by providing equal access to information

Our Aim

It's quite simple, let’s make document accessibility easy for organizations to achieve, one accessible document at a time!

Our Process for Document Remediation

Each document is being processed through Adobe Acrobat accessibility checker, the PDF Accessibility Checker 3, and CommonLook PDF Validator.

  1. Section 508 & WCAG 2.0 AA standards Applied
  2. Documents are evaluated for accessibility using adobe acrobat.
  3. We manually verify the tag structure using several programs to ensure PDF/UA standards.
  4. Heading structure applied throughout document
  5. Tag Structure verified and manually tested
  6. Alternate Text set for all images, charts, and graphs.
  7. Table Headers for all tables
  8. Link embedding and verification
  9. Lists
  10. Logical Reading Order manually verified
